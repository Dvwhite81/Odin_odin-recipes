Fettucini Alfredo

Description

Noodles, butter, flour, garlic, milk, hot sauce, and parmesan cheese. If you like thinner noodles, use linguine noodles.

Ingredients

  • Dry Fettucini Pasta
  • Butter
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Milk
  • Garlic
  • Hot Pepper Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water.
  2. Meanwhile, melt butter or margarine in a saucepan.
  3. Stir in flour.
  4. Whisk in milk, garlic, and hot sauce. Stir until thickened.
  5. Stir in cheese.
  6. Drain pasta. Serve sauce over noodles.
