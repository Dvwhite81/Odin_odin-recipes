Turkey Tetrazzini

Description

Turkey Tetrazzini is a quick and easy way to turn leftover turkey into a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • Egg Noodles
  • Chopped Cooked Turkey
  • Cream of Celery Soup
  • Sour Cream
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add noodles and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  4. Meanwhile, melt butter in a large heavy skillet. Drain mushrooms, add to skillet, and cook, stirring, for 1 minute.
  5. Stir in turkey, condensed soup, sour cream, salt, and pepper.
  6. Drain noodles and place in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Pour turkey mixture evenly over the top. Sprinkle with Parmesan.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until sauce is bubbling, 20 to 25 minutes.
